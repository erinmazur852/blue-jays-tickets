Last updated: June 12, 2026

What we collect

Nothing. This site has no accounts, no sign-up, no forms, no analytics, and no advertising trackers. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Local storage

If you set target prices on the dashboard, they are saved in your own browser's local storage so they persist between visits. That data never leaves your device and we cannot see it. Clearing your browser data removes it.

Third-party links

Buy links lead to independent ticket marketplaces. Once you leave this site, their privacy policies apply. Some outbound links may be affiliate links, which let the destination marketplace know the visit came from this site; this involves no personal information from us, because we hold none.

Hosting

The site is served by Vercel and GitHub Pages, which may log standard technical request data (such as IP addresses) to operate their services. See their respective privacy policies for details.
