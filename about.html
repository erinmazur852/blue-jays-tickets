This site tracks the lowest resale prices for Toronto Blue Jays home games at Rogers Centre, overall and by stadium section, in Canadian dollars. Prices are checked every 30 minutes and the full price history is kept, so fans can see whether today's price is actually a deal for that matchup and section before they buy.

How it works

The game schedule comes from the official MLB schedule API. Resale prices are sampled from public marketplace listings and converted to CAD at the Bank of Canada daily rate. Set a target price on any game or section and the dashboard flags it when the price drops to your number.

Buying tickets

This site does not sell tickets. The buy links go to established ticket marketplaces where the transaction, delivery, and buyer guarantees are handled entirely by that marketplace. Some links may be affiliate links, which pay this site a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Affiliation
